26 May

French Open Grand Slam

The French Open, also known as Roland Garros, is one of the four Grand Slam tennis tournaments. It takes place annually in late May and early June at the Stade Roland Garros in Paris, France.

3 Jul

Wimbledon Championships

Wimbledon is the oldest tennis tournament in the world and is widely considered the most prestigious. It is held at the All England Club in Wimbledon, London, UK, usually in early July.

31 Aug

US Open

The US Open is the final Grand Slam tournament of the year. It is held annually in Flushing Meadows, New York, during late August and early September.

20 Jan

Australian Open

The Australian Open kicks off the Grand Slam calendar in Melbourne, Australia. It usually takes place in mid to late January and is known for its intense summer heat.

Early Beginnings

Tennis has a long history, with origins dating back to the 12th century. The game was originally played with the hand, which is how it got its name, derived from the French word "tenez," meaning "to take" or "receive."

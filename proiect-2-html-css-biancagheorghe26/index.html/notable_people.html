Serena Williams

Serena Williams is a legendary tennis player known for her powerful serve and numerous Grand Slam titles.

  • Won 23 Grand Slam singles titles
  • Gold medalist at the Olympics

Roger Federer

Roger Federer is one of the greatest tennis players of all time with a graceful playing style.

  • 20 Grand Slam singles titles
  • Held the world No. 1 spot for a record 310 weeks
